# Privacy Policy Template

This template is a starting point for publishing the app to Google Play. It is not legal advice and should be reviewed for your business before use.

## Offline Invoice Privacy Policy

Offline Invoice is an offline-first invoice application. The app is designed to store invoice data on the device and does not include analytics, ad tracking, or a custom backend service.

## Data We Process

- Business profile details you enter, such as name, phone number, email, and address.
- Client records and invoice data you create in the app.
- Optional purchase status used to unlock Pro features through Google Play Billing.
- Optional local backup files that you export or import.

## How Data Is Used

- To create, store, and manage invoices on your device.
- To generate PDF invoices and CSV exports that you choose to share.
- To restore your Pro purchase status when you request a purchase restore.

## Data Sharing

- We do not sell your data.
- We do not send your invoice database to a custom backend controlled by the app developer.
- When you choose to share a PDF, CSV, message, email, or backup file, the selected Android app receives the file you chose to share.
- In-app purchases are processed by Google Play and are subject to Google's own terms and privacy practices.

## Permissions

The Android app may request:

- Contacts access, if you use the contact picker to add a client from device contacts.
- Network access, for Google Play Billing and platform services required for purchase validation and restore.
- Storage and document access only when you choose to import, export, or share files.
- Vibrate, for standard device feedback.

`SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW` is used only in debug development builds and is not included in the Play release build.

## Data Retention

Your business data remains on the device unless you export or share it. Removing the app may delete locally stored data unless you created a backup first.
